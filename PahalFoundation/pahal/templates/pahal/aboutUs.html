Introduction

Who We Are

We are a community of passionate university students who believe that our academic privilege is a responsibility. Based at VIT Bhopal University, we identify the children of campus workers, daily-wage laborers, and surrounding village communities who lack access to consistent, quality schooling.

Pahal is 100% student-managed. Every "Pahalite" contributes their unique skills in technology, management, and pedagogy to provide a professional foundation for our kids. We aren't just teaching; we are building a support system that fosters confidence and emotional intelligence.

Our Roots at VIT Bhopal

Operating within the heart of Central India, our foundation leverages the technical and creative talent of the VIT student body. We transform empty classrooms into hubs of hope, ensuring that the progress of the university also means the progress of its neighboring communities.

The Purpose

Why We Do What We Do

In the shadows of modern development, millions of children remain trapped in a cycle where they must start working before they learn to read. At Pahal, we refuse to accept this as the final reality.

Breaking Cycles Literacy is the only permanent exit from generational poverty.
Equal Access Mentorship shouldn't be a luxury reserved for the elite.
Community Growth An educated child uplifts the aspirations of an entire village.
The Evolution

Our Story

What began in 2021 as a small circle of friends teaching under a tree at VIT Bhopal has evolved into a structured foundation. We realized that the world was waiting for us right outside our dormitories.

2021
The First Step

Pahal was founded by a group of VITians with 5 volunteers and 10 curious kids under a tree near the campus gate.

2024
Scaling Impact

Expansion to neighboring villages like Kothri Kalan with a structured weekend and morning curriculum.

Today
The Movement

50+ Mentors providing daily 1-on-1 guidance, digital literacy, and personal development to over 100+ students.

How We Work

Our Impact Strategy

Our presence at VIT Bhopal allows us to use a multi-dimensional approach to empower local children.

Daily Morning Classes

Regular sessions on campus from 10 AM to 12:30 PM focusing on basic literacy, school catch-up, and math logic.

Health & Hygiene

Leveraging local resources for health checkups and providing workshops on sanitation and nutrition.

Creative Workshops

Using the diverse talents of VIT students to teach art, drama, and sports to build public speaking skills.

Our Mission

To identify every child in the Sehore/Bhopal periphery who is out of school and provide them with the materials, mentorship, and motivation required to lead a life of dignity.

Our Vision

To create a replicable student-led NGO model starting from VIT Bhopal that can be adopted by universities nationwide to empower their local communities.
